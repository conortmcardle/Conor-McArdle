// about

About

Peddie School is one of the nation's leading independent college-preparatory boarding schools. I managed the production of all multimedia in support of the school's brand, serving as staff photographer and videographer while directing external vendors for signature deliverables.

// what i did

What I Did

  • Strategized, directed, shot, and edited films that told the Peddie story in a compelling, consistent visual format across all mediums
  • Designed graphics for social media and advertising
  • Managed social media for school accounts
  • Managed vendor relationships for high-level production deliverables
  • Co-led the redesign of peddie.org in partnership with Electric Kite

// why it mattered

Why It Mattered

This role was a bridge between education and full-time institutional communications — the place where my classroom instincts and my production skills converged into a focused professional identity. It sharpened my ability to think strategically about brand, audience, and story at the institutional level.
