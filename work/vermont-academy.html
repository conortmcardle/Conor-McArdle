// about

About

Vermont Academy is a small independent boarding school in southern Vermont. For six years, I taught Filmmaking, Graphic Design, and Digital Photography to high school students while also serving as a dorm parent, multi-sport coach, and student adviser.

// teaching

Teaching

I designed and taught courses in filmmaking, graphic design, and digital photography — building curricula from scratch and mentoring students through hands-on creative projects. The work went beyond the classroom: I coached multiple sports, lived on campus as a dorm parent, and served as a student adviser. In 2016, the school community awarded me the Barbara Sweet Teacher of the Year Award.

// communications

Communications

Alongside teaching, I served as an assistant to the Communications Office, creating video, photography, design, and promotional content for the school. I also designed the yearbook for all six years. This dual role — educator and communicator — taught me how to listen to an institution's identity and translate it into visual stories that connect.

Yearbook samples, video work, photography, and course materials coming soon.
